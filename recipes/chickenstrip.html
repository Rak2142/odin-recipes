Chicken Strips

At the end of the day, wouldn't you much rather just give them, kids, husband, wife, or all of the above, what they want? Nothing beats an all time favorite, crispy breaded chicken strips, paired with our honey mustard sauce.

Marinated in buttermilk, seasoned to perfection and fried to crispy perfection. A versatile dish that goes great on its own, served on a salad or finger food for the game day.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chicken tenderloin
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 1.5 cup all purpose flour
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • 1 heaping tsp salt
  • 3/4 tsp black pepper
  • 3/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 3/4 tsp paprika
  • 3 tbsp buttermilk
  • 3 -4 cups vegetable oil, depending on side of pan

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken, place tenders in a bag with buttermilk, paprika, garlic powder, cayenne pepper, and salt. Mix contents in bag until contents are evenly distributed. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  2. Combine flour, baking powder, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.Whisk until well blended.
  3. Add buttermilk and mix with a fork till the mixture is evenly clumpy.
  4. Place tenders into the breading and coat. Remember to use one hand for wet side, and the other for dry side. It will get messy.
  5. Heat oil till at 350 degrees F. Place chicken tenders into the oil without crowding the pan. Cook for a few minutes then flip. Ensure both sides are golden brown.
  6. Set tenders on a paper towel lined baking sheet to drain.
  7. Once all are made, serve with Honey Mustard and enjoy!
