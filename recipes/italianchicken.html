Zesty Italian Chicken

This chicken recipie will have you coming back for seconds. Making 4 servings and taking less than an hour, you can feed the family without spending all day in the kitchen.

Easy prep, especially if you use pre prepared ingredients, along with the simple marinade, Zesty itilian dressing. You can feed your loved ones after a short time in the oven!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Your favorite Italian Dressing
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 packet zesty italian seasoning mix
  • 1 pound baby potatoes, halved (or cut into bite-sized pieces)
  • 1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
  • Salt and pepper; to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Line with aluminum foil, spray with cooking spray.
  2. Add chicken breast with generous amount of Italian dressing, make sure chicken is entirely coated. Let marinade for a couple hours.
  3. Melt butter in a bowl, add lemon juice and italian seasoning mix.
  4. Toss potatoes and green beans in butter mixture. Spread on an even layer on one side of the sheet pan. Drizzle any remaining mixture over the top.
  5. Arrange chicken on the opposite side of pan in a single layer.
  6. Salt and pepper everything to taste.
  7. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, until chicken is cooked through and potatoes are tender.
  8. Let chicken rest for a few minutes, then slice and serve with veggies.
