Homemade Bread Loaf

What is better than the smell of bread baking? With this recipe you wont need to get bread from the store anymore. With just a few simple ingredients you will be making your own bread like a pro.

Create your own perfect loaf of bread, that will handle your thickest sandwich and will stay soft for days ready for you to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup + 3 tbps Milk (280g)
  • 2 tbsp Sugar (30g)
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil (30g)
  • 2 tsp Yeast (7g)
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 3 cups Strong Bread Flower (420g) (13% protein)

Steps

  1. Warm milk add salt, sugar, olive oil, and yeast. Stir till combined
  2. Add flour into mixer, gently add milk mixture to the flower.
  3. Mix on medium speed till dough comes together. Increase speed to high and kneed for 3 minutes.
  4. Grease hands and a bowl, then shape dough into a ball.
  5. Cover dough and allow to rise for 2 hours. Dough should triple in size.
  6. Grease a loaf pan.
  7. Roll dough into a rectangle, use your pan for reference.
  8. Roll dough into a tube shape then place into your pan seam side down. (You can press gently down on the dough for a more even rise)
  9. Cover and allow to rise for 1 hour, or until the dough has risen above the top of your loaf pan.
  10. Bake bread in 350 degrees F for 25 minutes. (If you overbake the bread will be dry.)
  11. Optional: While bread is still hot, you may butter the top.
  12. Allow to cool for 1 hour before you can cut and enjoy your bread.
