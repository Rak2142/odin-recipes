Homemade Honey Mustard

Who doesn't love a good dipping sauce? This honey mustard will compliment our chicken tender recipe nicely, as well as any meat dishes you want to try it with. With its sweet, savory, and creamy flavors you will have the whole neighborhood asking where you get it.

If you check the ingredients for any store bought sauce it probably has quite a few things that you have never even heard of before. Our recipe takes just a few ingredients and is very simple to make. Once you give it a try, you'll never go back to store bought.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1/8 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/8 tsp paprika
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • fresh cracked pepper

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a bowl then whisk or stir till smooth.
  2. Enjoy
